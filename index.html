About

I am a Ph.D. student at George Mason University, specializing in Ethical AI within the Computer Science department. My research focuses on developing and testing machine learning fairness tools and debugging methods, particularly using fuzzy testing. This work addresses the growing need for AI systems that are both fair and reliable, ensuring they align with societal values and contribute positively to our technological landscape. By enhancing the robustness of these tools, my research aims to make a significant impact on the field and broader society.
